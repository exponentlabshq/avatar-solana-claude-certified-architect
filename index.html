Aristotle's Framework

The Four Primary Causes — Your Navigation Map

Every CCA domain reduces to one of these four questions. Master the causes, and distractor answers dissolve on sight.

ὕλη

Material Cause

"What is it made of?"

Tokens, the context window, and conversation history are the substrate every Claude system runs on — Katara's waterbending is the art of managing what flows through that container across turns.

Domain 5 — Context Management
εἶδος

Formal Cause

"What is its defining pattern?"

The system prompt and CLAUDE.md constitute what Claude IS — identity, persona, permissions — just as Iroh constitutes the conditions for Zuko's growth without prescribing each step.

Domains 3 & 4 — Config & Prompts
κίνησις

Efficient Cause

"What initiates change?"

Hooks, tools, subagents, and MCP servers — PreToolUse, PostToolUse, agentic loops — are what sets Claude in motion; the bending that produces action in the world.

Domains 1 & 2 — Agents & Tools
τέλος

Final Cause

"What is it for?"

Termination conditions — stop_reason values like end_turn, tool_use, max_tokens — define what the system is aimed at; Zuko's corrective arc is the lived lesson in telos gone wrong, then righted.

Domain 1 — Termination & Alignment
⚠

The Formal↔Efficient Swap — the most dangerous error on the exam and in production. Treating the system prompt (εἶδος, what Claude IS) as if it were a hook (κίνησις, what sets Claude in motion) is how impressive architects build the wrong thing with impressive technique. Hold this distinction from page one.

Guide Personas

Iroh the Mentor & Zuko the Corrective Arc

Two voices carry you through the guide — one sets the constituting ground, one walks the path so you can learn from its costly detours.

Uncle Iroh

Formal Cause (εἶδος) · Mentor · Air & Tea

Iroh does not specify each step you must take. He constitutes the conditions — the tea, the patience, the White Lotus framework — in which correct steps become possible. This is second-order pedagogy: the system prompt as constituting ground, not execution script.

His teaching holds that fire is life, not destruction — an insight attributed in legend to the ancient dragons themselves, whose nature is said to embody flame as creative rather than merely destructive force. Whether or not the full story of his enlightenment is as tradition tells it, the architectural truth stands: what defines a system's nature is always deeper than the commands that activate it.

Prince Zuko

Final Cause (τέλος) · Corrective Arc · Fire Nation

Zuko's arc is technically impressive execution aimed at the wrong goal — and then, painfully, corrected. This is the Arthas Anti-Pattern and its resolution: maximum capability, precision technique, wrong τέλος.

The full arc is the lesson. Zuko does not merely illustrate the executor role — he demonstrates that architectural alignment is a moral and structural achievement, not a configuration detail. You, the Practitioner, inherit the corrective arc: the path from impressive technique toward the right goal.
